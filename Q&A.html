Questions and Answers

why should i log in?

You can play without logging in, but your score won't be saved if you are not signed in. So, if you want to see yourself on the ranking board, you better be logged in

how to play?

You just need to click the 'Start' button and then use the 'Space' key on the keyboard. However, if you are logging in, the game will start automatically after you click the 'Log In' button.

how can i see my score?

Your score will be displayed on the leaderboard on the ranking page.
